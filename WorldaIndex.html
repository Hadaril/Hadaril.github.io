Hadaril's "NextGen" OTL Worlda Maps

The NextGen OTL WorldA Project is an ongoing project to modernize, standardize, and unify alternatehistory.com's collection of historical "WorldA" maps.

Prior to this project, the community's collection of maps was a haphazard mess. Each map was built on a different edition of the WorldA template, utilizing a disjointed mess of color schemes ranging from UCS to TACOS. Borders were all over the place, wildly inaccurate, and highly inconsistent.

This project remedied those issues. As of June 2020 (3 years after I launched the project), all of the existing WorldA maps have been updated to a new universal template. Color schemes were standardized with the new NCS (NextGen Color Scheme). Massive improvements were made to the quality and historical accuracy of the maps.

I began work on revisions in the winter of 2017, and as of June 2020, I have fully revised all of the existing WorldAâ€™s, and made dozens more from scratch. It took well over 300 hours of work, but the results were worth it. As of now, The NextGen Worlda Series is the most complete series of historical maps on the forum, with maps spanning from prehistory to the modern age.

In this series:

  • All maps are drawn on the most recent edition of the blank worlda
  • Utilize a standard and consistant color scheme (Dubbed the "NextGen Color Scheme", NCS for short)
  • Borders remain consistant and accurate over time.

Major thanks to everyone who has contributed their own patches and regional revisions to the project. Special thanks go to LSCatalina, CulturalDelusionists, Salem, Viralworld, Bob Hope, ST15RM, Thanosaekk, James the AH Fan, and Admiral A. Kolchak.
